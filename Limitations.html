General Limitations

  • Google allows you to create limited number of files in 24 hours (250-1,500, depends on your G-Suite plan). You can check your account limitation here.

  • Google allows functions to run up to 6-30 minutes each time (also depends on your G-Suite plan).
    Stopping functions by google server may cause lose of data.
    One form creation / analysis take 5-20 seconds, so be careful!
